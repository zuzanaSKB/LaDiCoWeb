Varenna


Varenna, nestled on Lake Como's eastern shore in Italy, is a charming, must-visit village known for its peacefulness and authenticity.

This small town holds onto traditions of the past, especially fishing. It's home to elegant villas like Villa Monastero and Villa Cipressi, along with scenic walks like Castle of Vezio and Sentiero del Viandante that blend history and nature.

If you arrive in Varenna by ferry, you'll likely start near the romantic Passeggiata degli Innamorati (Walk of Lovers) with a stunning view of the lake. As you stroll, you'll notice charming streets leading into the heart of the village, which is lined with characteristic restaurants, cozy bars, and artisan shops. The everlasting charm of Varenna captivates the soul.
